Waffle the Pup

Waffle was rescued in April of 2019 by his human, Mallory. They live in New York City and absolutely love it there. Waffle's birthday is March 13th and he will be 4 years old in 2022. He loves to explore Central Park, go on walks, get treats, and hang out with his human. He is a chihuahua/dacschund mix and weighs about 10 lbs.
