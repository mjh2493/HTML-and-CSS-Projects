Waffle the Pup

Waffle is a rescue dog living in New York City with his human, Mallory.

Waffle Facts:

He loves to play ball, but he is not very good at bringing it back.

He was rescued from Georgia, where his human happens to be from!

While he is cute, he has a lot of behavior issues. You can learn more about them and how his human trains him on this website.

He is a city dog through and through. He loves living in New York City and going on adventures.
